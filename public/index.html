Lonely Astronaut

Directions

You are an astronaut floating in space who has unfortunately stumbled into a stampede of asteroids. Evade the asteroids with your mouse to avoid your inevitable demise. The longer you survive, the higher your score. The game ends when your lives counter hits 0. You can enhance your lonely astronaut experience using the control panel on the right. If you want to restart, press the play button in right top corner.
