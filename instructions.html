Livestock Production by Region Instructions


  • Select an animal on the legend to only view the statistics about that animal. Warning, if the information is too small for the Y-axis, the graph may appear empty. Select a different scaling option (explained below)
  • To change the scale of the graph, select a gray circle on the right hand side of the graph. Any data that exceeds the scale will appear more transparent. Note the change in Y-axis.
  • Hover over any bar to see the production details on the right, under the graph scaling options
  • To restore the settings to the default, click "R" on your keyboard
  • To bring up additional information about this dataset, click "B" on your keyboard
  • To open these instructions again, click "?" on your keyboard
Enjoy!
