Welcome to the Audio Visualizer!

This audio visualizer displays three groups of frequencies: lows (bass), mids, and highs (treble), shown in visual elements from bottom to top, respectively.

Controls

On the right, you will find the visualizer controls. There are three tracks available, which may be selected using the "Track" dropdown. The Play/Pause controls the playback of the selected track.

There are three sliders: Playback speed, volume, and "Pulse." The first two are self-explanatory. "Pulse" controls how much the visualizer circles increase in size when the are displaying frequencies.

The colors folder allows you to pick the color for the three visualizer groups.
