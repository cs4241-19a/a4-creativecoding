Audio Visualization

Created by Elie Hess

Usage:

Click the Play button to start playing the song.
Click the Pause button to pause the song.
Click the Stop button to pause the song and reset it to the beginning.
Click the Help button to pause the song and redisplay this message.
While the audio is playing, click the "Open Controls" button at the top right to control the visualization.
While the audio is playing, click the "Color" button to cycle through blue, green, and red visuals

The song playing is "Caracas" by Tintamare
