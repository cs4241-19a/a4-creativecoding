About IA

IA is a VOCALOID, a humanoid persona voiced by a singing synthesizer application developed by 1st Place Co., Ltd. People can use IA's voice bank to create their own songs.

The songs provided were created by Soundcloud user, Kita-Kei, and are being shared through a Creative Commons License.

Welcome

Hello! To customize the music settings, please refer to the top right. You can select the song, how the music sounds, and how the stereo bars look. You can play, pause, and resume from the buttons on the left side. If you change the song, you will need to replay.
